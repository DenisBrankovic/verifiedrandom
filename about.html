About VerifiedRandom.org



Our mission is to provide free, verified, truly random numbers for anyone to utilize for any legal purpose they may have.

VerifiedRandom.org was made in response to the costs associated with other paid platforms that require a monthly fee or per-draw payment.

We do not provide 3rd party draw services. Contact us with any questions and we will respond as soon as possible.
